Why nl-veil?

Designed for developers who want intelligent automation without surrendering their data or infrastructure.

⚙

Local-First Architecture

Every mind runs on your hardware. No data leaves your machine unless you explicitly choose to connect an API.

★

Emergent Hive Intelligence

Cast swarms of specialized sub-agents that collaborate and self-organize — each mind contributes unique capabilities.

⚡

Speed-First Execution

Parallel task distribution, automatic time budgets, and built-in orchestration. Your swarms converge fast.

🛠

Tool-Integrated Builder

Hands-on file tools, terminal access, and persistent memory. Build, test, and deploy without leaving the chat.

Quick Start

Zig npm Docker
